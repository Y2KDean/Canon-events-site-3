Who we are

We specialize in luxury event consulting and planning, handling everything “from permits to proposals.” Utilizing AI-driven workflows. We optimize your workflow and assit with automation of benign tasks, leaving the bespoke tasks up to you. We manage an in-house staffing agency, born in NYC & the Hamptons, we are trusted across top-tier markets nationwide. From polished servers and bartenders to full-scale operational teams, our wonderful staff elevate any occasion.

Whether it’s an intimate estate soirée in Bridgehampton, a brand launch in Beverly Hills, or a waterfront gala in Palm Beach, our approach blends sophistication, discretion, and seamless execution.

With a footprint spanning New York City, South Florida, Texas, Nashville, Scottsdale, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, and the Hamptons, Canon Events brings East Coast polish and nationwide versatility to every experience we touch.

Our Services

"From Permits to Proposals"

All-in-One Staffing Solutions

We offer complete staffing coverage for events — including model-caliber servers, bartenders, captains, hosts, and brand reps. Whether you need just one server or a full crew, we deliver polished, professional teams trained to elevate guest experience across every detail.

Permit Expediting

From tent permits to local compliance with alcohol and noise ordinances, we handle the red tape so your event never skips a beat — especially in high-regulation markets like the Hamptons, Los Angeles, etc.

Workflow Optimization

We work behind the scenes to streamline your internal event operations — helping planners, caterers, and venues build smarter systems, improve staffing flow, and automate redundant tasks using pre-built AI models.

Virtual Assistance

Our Event Operations Assistants (EOAs) provide remote support to busy planners and vendors. From managing proposals and timelines to communicating with clients and vendors, your dedicated assistant ensures nothing slips through the cracks.

Multi-Market Expertise

Operating in South Florida, Texas, Nashville, Scottsdale, Las Vegas, Los Angeles, and based in the Hamptons, we bring local compliance expertise, regional style fluency, and scalable teams ready to meet the moment, wherever your event happens.
